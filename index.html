What is FRP (Factory Reset Protection)?

Factory Reset Protection (FRP) is a security feature introduced by Google that prevents someone from using your Android device after a factory reset — unless they can verify the last Google account that was synced to it.

If you've forgotten your Google account credentials after a reset, or purchased a second-hand device that's locked, our professional FRP Bypass service will get your device back up and running — safely and legally.
